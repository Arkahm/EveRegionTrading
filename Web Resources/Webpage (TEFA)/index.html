TEFA might be right for you!

You are probably asking yourself, "Who the heck is TEFA"? TEFA are The Engineers for Annihilation. We were formed by four friends that love the game Eve Online. We wanted to find a way to make in game money (ISK) to support PvP, improve programming knowledge, and just plain challenge ourselves to prove it could be done easily without much risk.

Starting with a pathetic 800 Million ISK between the 4 of us (200 Million each), we explored the path of regional market trading. We found our niche. With one of us scripting and the rest buying, selling, and transporting we have expanded that business model and are now ready to expand further, accepting applications for employees. This IS the way to go when making those ISKies.
